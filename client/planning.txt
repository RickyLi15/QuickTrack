Homepage - introduces client to the web app, provides the client with links
to plan their workout and an instruction page.


Instruction page - describe to the client what exactly this app is about.



planning page - links to different parts of an exercise such as upper body,
lower body, core, etc. Each one of these pages contain specific workouts that the 
user can add to their day 


statistics page - displays the information about a certain client, such 
as total calories burned, height, and weight
